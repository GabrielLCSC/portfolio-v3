UnlockM

UnlockM is a platform for B2B marketing leaders, offering peer-to-peer learning and development through courses and networking opportunities. It provides comprehensive marketing training programs designed by top industry experts, focusing on practical strategies and real-world applications. This project was developped in NextJs supported with Storyblok.

Amaclio

Amaclio Productions creates, produces, and stages prestigious multimedia shows that highlight significant historical sites in France. Their expertise spans conception, production, realization, and management of events, aiming to offer audiences immersive and memorable experiences. Some of their notable works include "La Nuit aux Invalides" and "Les Nocturnes de l'Abbaye." Project also developped in NextJs with Storyblok as a CMS.

Second Step

Second Step is an online store specializing in high-quality, authenticated second-hand sneakers. They offer a wide range of refurbished sneakers from popular brands like Nike, Adidas, and Yeezy, ensuring both sustainability and affordability. The platform emphasizes reducing waste by giving sneakers a second life and providing a flexible, secure payment system along with free delivery. Realised using NextJs and Storyblok.

My personal projects !

PokeSearch (responsive)

This project has been made in reactJs. The aim was to consume an api (here pokeApi) and to display the info. To make it more complex, I added features such as a search bar, and a favorites page. It's my favorite project so far !

Parallax effect (desktop only)

This project has recently been completed. The aim was to create a technical parallax effect for a computer user. The site loads a little slowly because of the weight of the images. Optimisations are in the pipeline to solve this problem !
